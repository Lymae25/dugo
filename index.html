Born Davao. Built back. Bloodline

Minimal form. Concrete palette. One mark that carries the story through the body.

Two places. One body.

DUGO is for people who carry the place they came from — and the place they became — at the same time.

ORIGIN

Return is a material.

Cabantian isn’t a moodboard. It’s heat, dust, unfinished hollow-block walls. DUGO translates that texture into clothing.

INTENT

Quiet confidence.

MODEL

SIGNATURE
